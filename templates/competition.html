Photography Competition

Welcome to the Photography Competition! This is an exciting opportunity for photographers all around the world to showcase their talent and creativity. Whether you are a professional photographer or an amateur looking to make your mark, this competition is open to everyone.

The Photography Competition is an event to find the best photographs across various genres and themes. We invite photographers from all over the world to participate. The competition is open to all, and participants can submit their photos to compete for amazing prizes!

Participation Requirement

To participate, you must take a membership which costs NRS 300(USD $2.16). Only those who have registered with the membership can submit their photos for the competition. The prize pool will be determined based on the number of participants. The more members who join, the larger the prize pool!

How to Participate

To take part in this exciting competition, follow these simple steps:

  • Sign Up: Take the membership by paying NRS 300(USD $2.16). After registration, you will receive details on how to submit your photographs.
  • Submit Your Photograph: Upload your photo to the competition platform. Your photograph will be shared with the public for voting.
  • Voting & Judging: Judging will be based on the highest number of likes received on your photograph. The photograph with the highest number of likes at the end of the competition will win the top prize.

Winning Criteria

The participant with the most liked photograph wins the competition. Encourage your friends, family, and followers to support your photo by liking it!

Rules & Regulations

To ensure a fair and smooth competition, please follow the rules and regulations:

  • Eligibility: The competition is open to everyone worldwide. You must take the membership NRS 300(USD $2.16) to participate.
  • Submissions: Only one photograph per person is allowed. Photos must be your own work and must not have been submitted to any other competition before.
  • Judging Criteria: The winner will be chosen based on the number of likes a photograph receives. The photograph with the highest number of likes will be declared the winner.
  • Disqualification: Any attempt to manipulate the number of likes (such as using bots or fake accounts) will result in disqualification.
  • Public Voting: The number of likes on your submission will be visible to everyone. Participants are encouraged to share their submission and ask their networks to vote.
  • Photo Integrity: No one is allowed to take a screenshot of your photograph or use developer tools to manipulate the image on the page. Any attempt to alter the competition mechanics will result in disqualification.
  • Submission Deadline: All submissions must be completed by the specified deadline. Late submissions will not be considered.

Additional Information

  • Future Competitions: If we receive enough participation, we will be organizing additional competitions, such as Videography Competitions and Gaming Tournaments. Stay tuned for updates!
  • Announcement of Winners: The winner will be announced once the competition ends, and the prize will be awarded accordingly. The announcement will be made through our official channels.
  • Privacy: Participants agree that their photos may be featured on our website and social media platforms as part of the competition process.

Good Luck!

We wish you the best of luck in the competition! Let your creativity shine, and may the best photographer win!
